INVESTMENT CONTRACT

Boostify Music - Strategic Investor Program

This INVESTMENT CONTRACT (hereinafter, the "Contract") is entered into on .

BETWEEN:

BOOSTIFY MUSIC, an entity registered under applicable laws, with its principal office at 123 Music Avenue, Miami, FL 33101, United States (hereinafter, the "Company").

AND

The person or entity accepting this Contract (hereinafter, the "Investor").

Both parties, hereinafter jointly referred to as the "Parties".

1. PURPOSE OF THE CONTRACT

1.1. The purpose of this Contract is to establish the terms and conditions under which the Investor will make a financial investment in the Company, with the aim of contributing to the growth and development of the Boostify Music platform and its related services.

1.2. The Company is engaged in the development and operation of an advanced technological platform for the music industry, providing creation, promotion, and management tools for artists, using artificial intelligence technologies.

2. INVESTMENT PLANS

2.1. The Investor agrees to make an investment in the Company according to one of the following plans:

Plan Details Standard Plan Premium Plan Elite Plan
Risk Level Low Risk Medium Risk High Potential
Monthly Return 4% 5% 6%
Minimum Investment $2,000 $5,000 $25,000
Minimum Term 6 months 12 months 18 months
Payment Frequency Monthly Monthly Monthly
Special Benefits Basic investor dashboard Access to exclusive events Participation in strategic decisions

2.2. In consideration for the investment made, the Investor will receive:

  • A digital investment certificate in Boostify Music.
  • Rights to participate in the returns generated as established in section 4.
  • Access to exclusive benefits of the Investors Program as detailed in section 5.

3. PAYMENT METHOD

3.1. The investment payment will be made by bank transfer to the account designated by the Company or through the electronic payment methods available on the platform.

3.2. Upon receipt of payment, the Company will issue proof of the investment made within a period not exceeding seven (7) business days.

4. INVESTMENT RETURNS

4.1. The Company commits to providing monthly returns according to the selected investment plan:

  • Standard Plan: 4% monthly return on the total invested amount.
  • Premium Plan: 5% monthly return on the total invested amount.
  • Elite Plan: 6% monthly return on the total invested amount.

4.2. Return payments will be made monthly, within the first five (5) business days of the following month.

4.3. The Company reserves the right to adjust return rates based on market conditions, with prior notice of at least thirty (30) days to the Investor.

5. EXCLUSIVE BENEFITS

5.1. In addition to financial returns, the Investor will have access to the following exclusive benefits based on their selected plan:

Standard Plan Benefits:

  • Access to the investor dashboard.
  • Monthly investment performance reports.
  • Priority customer support.

Premium Plan Benefits:

  • All Standard Plan benefits.
  • Invitations to exclusive industry events.
  • Special discounts on all Boostify Music premium services.
  • Quarterly strategy calls with the management team.

Elite Plan Benefits:

  • All Premium Plan benefits.
  • Participation in strategic decision-making meetings.
  • Early access to new platform features and services.
  • Personalized investment consulting with industry experts.
  • VIP access to music industry networking events.

6. DURATION

6.1. This Contract will have a minimum duration according to the selected investment plan:

  • Standard Plan: 6 months
  • Premium Plan: 12 months
  • Elite Plan: 18 months

6.2. After the initial period, the Investor may choose to:

  • Renew their participation under the conditions in effect at that time.
  • Request the liquidation of their investment as established in section 7.

7. INVESTMENT LIQUIDATION

7.1. After the minimum period has elapsed according to the selected plan, the Investor may request the total or partial liquidation of their investment, with a minimum advance notice of thirty (30) days.

7.2. The liquidation value will be calculated based on the Company's valuation at the time of the request, guaranteeing at minimum the return of the invested capital plus accrued returns.

7.3. Early liquidation requests (before the minimum term is completed) may be subject to an early withdrawal fee of up to 10% of the invested amount, depending on the time elapsed since the investment was made.

8. INFORMATION AND TRANSPARENCY

8.1. The Company commits to providing clear, truthful, and updated information about the business evolution, financial results, and any relevant circumstances that may affect the Investors' interests.

8.2. The Investor will have access to a personalized dashboard where they can view the progress of their investment in real-time.

9. CONFIDENTIALITY

9.1. All information provided to the Investor that is not in the public domain shall be confidential. The Investor agrees not to disclose such information without the prior written consent of the Company.

10. APPLICABLE LAW AND JURISDICTION

10.1. This Contract shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Florida, United States.

10.2. For the resolution of any dispute arising from the interpretation or execution of this Contract, the Parties submit to the jurisdiction of the Courts and Tribunals of Miami-Dade County, Florida, United States.
